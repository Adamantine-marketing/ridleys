Our Unique Branding Technology

How Your Brand Gets Sealed Inside Every Pen

A proprietary 4-step process that makes your branding indestructible. No sticker, no sleeve — sealed for life.

01
🖨️

360° Photo Print

A cylindrical plastic tube is wrapped with a 360° hi-definition photo-printed sheet featuring your brand name, logo, tagline, address and more.

02
🔭

Lens Magnification

The branded cylinder is inserted into the transparent pen body. The clear barrel acts as a precision lens, magnifying your branding beautifully.

03
🔒

Tamper-Proof Seal

Your branding is permanently sealed inside the pen body with a tamper-proof stopper — it can never be removed, peeled or scratched.

04
✨

Mini-Billboard Ready

Your indestructible, fade-proof, scratch-proof branding mini-billboard is complete and ready to represent your brand in every hand it reaches.
